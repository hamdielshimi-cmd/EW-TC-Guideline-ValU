Eye World & Top Care Brand Guidelines

Leadership Through Excellence in Healthcare

القيادة من خلال التميز في الرعاية الصحية

Since 2004, Eye World Hospital has established itself as a pioneering force in ophthalmology, delivering exceptional medical care to thousands of patients. Today, Eye World Hospital and Top Care Clinics represent a comprehensive healthcare ecosystem. Our success is built on three pillars: clinical expertise, patient-centered care, and continuous innovation.

منذ عام 2004، أثبتت مستشفى دنيا العيون نفسها كقوة رائدة في طب العيون، حيث قدمت رعاية طبية استثنائية لآلاف المرضى. اليوم، تمثل مستشفى دنيا العيون وعيادات توب كير نظاماً صحياً شاملاً. يقوم نجاحنا على ثلاث ركائز: الخبرة السريرية والرعاية المتمركزة حول المريض والابتكار المستمر.

✓ Typography Rules

  • Arabic text always appears first and above English

  • Do not use decorative fonts for medical content

  • Do not mix English and Arabic on same line

Social Media Guidelines إرشادات وسائل التواصل الاجتماعي

Eye World maintains a professional, educational presence across social platforms. Content focuses on clinical expertise, patient education, and building trust through consistent, quality messaging.

تحافظ دنيا العيون على حضور احترافي وتعليمي عبر منصات التواصل الاجتماعي. يركز المحتوى على الخبرة السريرية وتثقيف المريض وبناء الثقة من خلال الرسائل المتسقة والعالية الجودة.

Facebook
Posting Frequency: 4-5 times per week
Content Mix: 30% Education, 25% Expertise, 20% Conversion, 15% Community, 10% Behind-the-scenes
Caption Length: 150-250 characters optimal
Hashtags: 3-5 relevant hashtags, Arabic and English
Instagram
Posting Frequency: 3-4 times per week
Content Mix: 35% Education, 25% Expertise, 20% Visual storytelling, 20% Patient outcomes
Caption Length: 100-150 characters for main text
Hashtags: 15-25 hashtags (use first comment for additional hashtags)
YouTube

Subtitles: Arabic and English subtitles mandatory for all videos
WhatsApp Business

Message Format: Professional, bilingual support
Content: Appointment confirmations, health tips, special offers
Frequency: Maximum 2-3 messages per week to avoid spam

✓ Social Media Best Practices

  • Always include Arabic content first, English second
  • Use before/after photos with proper patient consent
  • Tag location and use relevant medical hashtags
  • Respond to comments within 24 hours
  • Include clear CTAs (Book Now, Learn More, Contact)

✗ Social Media Forbidden

  • Do not post patient photos without written consent
  • Do not make medical guarantees or promises
  • Do not engage in negative competitor commentary
  • Do not post graphic surgical images without warning
  • Do not share unverified medical information

Photography & Visual Standards معايير التصوير والمرئيات

Eye World photography emphasizes clinical precision, patient safety, and professional expertise. All imagery must convey trust, competence, and the human element of care.

يؤكد التصوير الفوتوغرافي في دنيا العيون على الدقة السريرية وسلامة المريض والخبرة المهنية. يجب أن تنقل جميع الصور الثقة والكفاءة والعنصر الإنساني للرعاية.

✓ Photography Approved

  • Real doctors and medical staff in professional settings
  • Modern, well-lit clinical environments
  • Advanced medical equipment and technology
  • Patient testimonials with written consent
  • Natural lighting, warm color tones
  • Before/after photos with proper disclaimers

✗ Photography Forbidden

  • Do not use stock photos of generic patients
  • Do not show graphic surgical procedures
  • Do not use patient photos without consent
  • Do not show outdated medical equipment
  • Do not use overly edited or filtered images
  • Do not show identifiable patient information

Legal Compliance:

الامتثال القانوني:

  • All patient photos require written informed consent with HIPAA compliance
  • Before/after photos must include medical disclaimer: "Results may vary by individual"
  • No identifiable patient information in captions or metadata
  • Maintain patient privacy per Egyptian Medical Board regulations
  • جميع صور المريض تتطلب موافقة مكتوبة مستنيرة مع الامتثال لـ HIPAA
  • يجب أن تتضمن صور قبل وبعد إخلاء مسؤولية طبية: "قد تختلف النتائج حسب الفرد"
